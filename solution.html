WHAT ABOUT IT?

After seeing how education is severely affected by different factors, it's now our turn to contribute to the solution.

Project Illuminare aims to share the situation of education around the world, and how each of us can help in making education accessible to all.

This crisis is a slow-motion catastrophe.

The lack of quality education translates into diminished human potential, dragging down global economic growth and innovation.

It fuels social instability by worsening inequality, undermining public health efforts, and eroding the foundational skills needed for an effective society.

With this, we must recognize the real problem. The failure to equip our students with the necessary skills and knowledge not only hampers individual growth but also stifles societal progress.

SO, WHAT CAN WE DO?

Organize volunteer networks of retired teachers, university students, and professionals to provide free, high-quality tutoring and homework assistance. This directly addresses the learning crisis by supplementing school resources and giving extra help to marginalized students, ensuring effective learning.

Launch fundraising drives that focuses on particular sectors of education, like infrastructure, books, materials, and many more. This can include, but not limited to supplying computers and reliable internet for digital literacy training, and building disability-sensitive, separate sanitation facilities to support the attendance of all students.

Advocacy: affordable and flexible education programs that teach different skills, like technical, vocational, digital, etc.This promotes the "lifelong learning opportunities for all" target, empowering people who missed out on formal education to secure decent work and contribute to economic growth.

"Words influence, and through us, we will be able to let the world know about how we can cater the world in our progression as a society."
